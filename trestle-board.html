About the Trestle Board

The Trestle Board is the monthly newsletter of Mizpah Lodge #302. It contains the Worshipful Master's message, upcoming events, lodge business summaries, and other announcements important to the brethren.

The easiest way to receive the Trestle Board is by email — subscribe above and each issue will arrive in your inbox.

Newsletter Archives

Past editions of the Trestle Board are being digitized and will be posted here as they become available. In the meantime, subscribe above to receive each new issue by email, or contact the secretary at Mizpah302sec@gmail.com to request a past issue.
