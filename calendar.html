Upcoming events and meetings at Mizpah Lodge #302

Regular Meetings

Mizpah Lodge #302 holds its stated meeting on the first Wednesday of each month at 7:00 PM at 1124 South 48th Street, Omaha, Nebraska.

Dinner is typically served prior to the meeting. All Master Masons in good standing are welcome to visit.
